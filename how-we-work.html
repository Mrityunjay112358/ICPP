HOW WE WORK

A Closed-Loop Model for Education Reintegration

We don't address one barrier at a time — we remove every barrier that keeps a child out of school.

01
🔁

Identify

We work within underserved communities to identify children who are out of school — often due to financial pressure, migration, or prolonged absence.

02
📚

Bridge Learning

Children attend daily doorstep learning sessions focused on literacy, numeracy, and life skills.

These programmes are designed for students who have been out of school for 1–3 years, helping them rebuild confidence and academic readiness.

03
🏠

Family Support

We work directly with families to address the root economic causes of school dropout.

This includes linking households to government welfare and income-support schemes, ensuring that education does not come at the cost of survival.

04
🏫

School Reintegration

Through partnerships with government schools and organisations, we create guaranteed pathways for children to re-enter formal education.

Students are placed into appropriate grade levels with support systems already in place.

05
🤝

Retention & Mentorship

Reintegration is not the end.

We provide continued mentorship, monitor attendance, and conduct sessions on mental health, resilience, and peer support — ensuring children stay in school and continue to progress.

06
🌱

System Strengthening

We also strengthen the ecosystem around the child by:

  • Supporting partner schools
  • Training youth volunteers
  • Integrating child-rights awareness into learning

This ensures long-term, sustainable impact beyond individual reintegration.

This model ensures that children don't just return to school — they remain there, supported by systems designed for their success.
